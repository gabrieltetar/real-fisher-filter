Textbooks on digital signal processing

My favourite textbooks on digital filter design are:
[Click on the ISBN for further information]

  • Rabiner, Lawrence R. & Gold, Bernard, Theory and application of digital signal processing, Prentice-Hall (1975), ISBN 0139141014
    One of the ``bibles'' in the field: over 20 years old, still highly relevant, and still in print!

  • Cunningham, Edward P., Digital filtering: an introduction, Houghton Mifflin (1992) / Wiley (1995), ISBN 0395539897 (H.M.), ISBN 0471124753 (Wiley)

  • Baher, H., Analogue & digital signal processing, Wiley (1990), ISBN 0471923427

  • Proakis, J.G. and Manolakis, D.G., Digital signal processing: principles, algorithms and applications, Macmillan (1996), ISBN 0133942899
A related book, not specifically on digital filter design:
[Click on the ISBN for further information]
  • Proakis, John G., Digital Communications, McGraw-Hill (1995), ISBN 0070517266
